# 🩺 Diabetes Health Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes the **Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) 2015** dataset containing over **253,000 survey responses**.

The dashboard helps identify relationships between diabetes status, lifestyle choices, demographics, and overall health indicators.

## 🎯 Business Questions

- Which age groups have the highest diabetes prevalence?
- How does BMI vary across diabetes categories?
- Is smoking associated with diabetes?
- How does physical activity affect diabetes prevalence?
- How do education and income influence diabetes outcomes?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

✅ Interactive filters

✅ Demographic segmentation

✅ Comparative analysis

✅ Health indicator analysis

✅ Lifestyle risk analysis

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Diabetes prevalence increases with age.
- Higher BMI is strongly associated with diabetes.
- Physical inactivity is more common among diabetic respondents.
- Individuals with diabetes report poorer general health.
- Education and income levels show noticeable differences across diabetes groups.
